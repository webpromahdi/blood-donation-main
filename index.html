Are You Eligible to Donate?

Most healthy adults can donate blood. Check these basic eligibility criteria to see if you qualify to become a blood donor.

Age between 18-65 years

Weight at least 50 kg (110 lbs)

Healthy and feeling well

No recent illness or infection

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about blood donation

Is blood donation safe?

Yes, blood donation is completely safe. Sterile, single-use equipment is used.

How often can I donate?

Healthy adults can donate every 3 months (whole blood donation).

Will it hurt?

You may feel a slight pinch, but most donors report minimal discomfort.
